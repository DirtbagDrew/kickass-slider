Features

  • Free
    KickAss is completely free (distributed under MIT License). However, if you appreciate good work, you can donate (by clicking 'Buy my time' button at footer). It aids to my work, time, life and my belief for MIT License ;)
  • Click & drag admin panel
    KickAss has been released as a wordpress plugin. The admin panel is designed beautifully & carefully, keeping wp's look & feel. Creating slides, adding images, choosing animations or adding text with WYSIWYG editor is as easy as pie & supported natively by wp core.
  • CSS3 transitions
    KickAss is powered with CSS3's awesomeness. No javascript / jQuery is used for animations (transitions). That means no flickering or uneven animation, even in the tablets and mobiles.
  • jQuery fallback on dumb browsers
    KickAss is developed keeping modern browsers (CSS3) in mind. But among all good, exists the evil. For browsers not supporting standards (You-Know-Who), jQuery fallbacks are deployed along with CSS3, to resonate the same effect in its absence.
